About

Lola Davis is a visionary fashion executive and entrepreneur with over a decade of experience building and scaling high-impact apparel brands.

She combines hands-on expertise in design, production, and supply chain management with strategic business acumen, consistently driving sustainable growth and market expansion.

As the founder of Lola Style Apparel, she successfully transformed a startup into a recognized contemporary women’s brand, achieving operational excellence, market penetration, and strong profitability.

Her leadership philosophy emphasizes ethical manufacturing, transparency, workforce development, and long-term brand value creation.
